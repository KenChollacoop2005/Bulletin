About me

My name is Ken. I have used a cane my entire life, and for as long as I can remember, I have walked into rooms and watched expectations quietly shift.

People are always a little surprised when the disabled kid is the one at the front of the room. That never sat right with me. Not because I want you to stop being surprised, but because I was never sure whether the surprise was for me, or in spite of me.
